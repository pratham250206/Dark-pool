Dark Pool Cipher

Find 7 target words hidden in the grid by dragging your cursor. But beware — the background letters scramble every 5 seconds!

How to Play

🖱️
Drag & Select

Click/touch and drag across adjacent letters in any of the 8 directions.

🌪️
Adaptive Matrix

Non-target letters shift every 5 seconds. Target words stay in place.

👁️
Sidebar List

Classic mode shows all words. Hidden mode keeps them hidden until found.

💡
Pro Hints

Stuck? Click Hint to flash the first letter of an unfound word on the board.
